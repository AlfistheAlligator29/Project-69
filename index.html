Campus Life

The Great Hall

Was established in 990 A.D.

The Great Hall is one of the most historical places in Hogwarts. It is where whiches and wizards eat there dinner, lunch and breakfast at. They also makes special announcments there and are places where the ghosts of hogwarts can hangout. It has banners hanging from the ceiling with the hogwarts logo on it and there are 5 tables. 4 for each of the 4 houses and the table for the staff and teaches. It's also a nice place to relax and study!

The Grand Staircase

Was established in 990 A.D.

The Grand staircase is one of the key places in Hogwarts as it leads to the House common rooms. It looks really cool as the stairs are interesting as they move randomly which makes them dangerous, but really cool. They feature alot of potraits of famous people in hogwarts such as, Helga Hufflepuff, Albus Dumbledore, Severous Snape and so much more! It is an interesting place to be and is very echoy!

The Qudditch Pitch

Was established in 1930

The Qudditch pitch is where qudditch matches happen or when important sporting events happen. The qudditch pitch is really big. It consists of 3 hoops on each side and 10 towers for spectating and for each house of the school including a special staff and teaches tower for spectating. It has alot of history as it has had many qudditch matches happen on it with some historic moments. A very important moment is when the tri-wizard tournement happened on the qudditch pitch and Hogwarts won!

Tri-Wizard Cup
Won in 1994/1995

In 1994/1995 Hogwarts held the tri-wizard tornament for witches and wizards. 4 champions were selected. The Hogwarts champions were Cedric Diggory and Harry Potter! Harry and Cedric won, but Cedric got murdered by Peter Pettigrew before returning back at home base. It was a joy to have a trophy, but a huge loss of a lovely Hogwarts student.



Qudditch Champions!
Won in 1997/1998

In 1997/1998 season, Hogwarts won the qudditch championships against Victor Krums team! The qudditch captain, Harry Potter and co-captain Ginny Weasly helped carry the team to victory as Ginny caught the golden snitch with seconds left on the clock! Everyone was overjoyed as the last time Hogwarts won the championship was in 1979 with James Potter as the captain! They celebrated like Kings and Queens!



MSOTY
Earned in 1996/1997

MSOTY stands for "Magical School of the Year"! After hosting the tri-wizard championship, the magical school department decided to make this a great school to go to and learn at. The scenery and education where deemed 10/10 and won the award for the next 4 years!
